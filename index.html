I'm interested in topics at the intersection of computation and mathematics. This has drawn me to projects of this flavor, recently centered around geometry processing, computational topology, physics-based simulation, linear algebra and optimization. Some recent projects include: fast distributed spatial queries, a parallel solver for elliptic partial differential equations, Bezier and spline algorithms, robust collision handling polynomial optimization, and optimal distance algorithms in the curve graph.
